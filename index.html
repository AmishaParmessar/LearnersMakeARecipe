How to make a delicious tropical smoothie

by Amisha Parmessar

Tropical smoothies are a delicious treats that are healthy. In this recipe I will show you a healthy tropical smoothies to make with a mix of fruits to show a variety amongst our favorite fruits. This is a healthy smoothie that you can drink anytime and it makes a perfect summer drink. Not only is it healthy but it is yummy! So be sure to follow these simple steps to make your day cheerful!

Recipe for Tropical Smoothie
Tropical Smoothie Ingredients Instructions
  1. Grab a blender and utensils
  2. Pick you favorite fruits such as banana, strawberries, cherries, blueberries, mangos. Have some fruits diced.
  3. Get some ice cubes
  4. Grab a jug of milk, juice or water
  5. (Optional: yougurt)
  • The first step is to grab a blender.
  • Add in your favorite fruits that you prefer.
  • Add it into the blender
  • Add in a glass of either milk, juice or water.
  • If you add a glass of milk, your smoothie will have a nice creamy texture to it.
  • Add in 10-15 ice cubes for a fresh and cool taste.
  • After you have everything added from the list of ingredients, you want to blend until you are satisfied with the texture of your smoothie.
  • Add you smoothie in a jar/jug, pour in some of the smoothie in you favorite glass.
  • Add in small diced fruits in the glass or on the rim of the glass (as shown in the picture above) and add a straw and enjoy your smoothie.
  • ENJOY THIS HEALTHY AND DELICIOUS TROPICAL SMOOTHIE.
